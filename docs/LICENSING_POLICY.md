# Licensing Policy

Asteria Star is open infrastructure. This policy covers how the knowledge graph,
datasets, and media are licensed.

## Knowledge graph & datasets

The structured knowledge graph and the datasets generated from it are offered
under **Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 4.0 (CC BY-SA 4.0)**.

- **Attribution** — credit "Asteria Star" and link back.
- **ShareAlike** — derivative datasets are shared under the same license.
- The license and version are embedded in every export (`/data/graph.json`,
  `/data/graph.jsonld`) and shown per dataset at `/datasets/{slug}`.

Facts themselves are not copyrightable; the license applies to the structured
compilation, descriptions, and relations.

## Imagery & media

Media is governed separately, per asset. The image platform records each image's
license — public domain, CC0, CC BY, CC BY-SA, or NASA/ESA media terms — with
credit and source. **No copyrighted or unlicensed imagery is bundled, and no
imagery is fabricated.** See [IMAGE_PLATFORM.md](./IMAGE_PLATFORM.md).

## Source material

Underlying facts come from authoritative sources (see
[SCIENTIFIC_SOURCES.md](./SCIENTIFIC_SOURCES.md)); their own terms apply to their
original materials. We reference and link rather than republish.

## Contributions

Future contributions are accepted under the same CC BY-SA 4.0 terms so the open
dataset remains coherent (see [CONTRIBUTION_STANDARDS.md](./CONTRIBUTION_STANDARDS.md)).
